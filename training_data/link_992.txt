The mission of the Medical Campus Media Services Department  is to serve the needs of faculty, administrators, staff and students with an  array of multimedia resources that enhance teaching and learning experiences,  campus marketing, and campus life. The Media Services Department provides  assistance with graphic design, event support, lecture capture, photography,  video, audio, and videoconferencing.
