Students can pick up a parking decal from the Student Life office located on campus in room 102. Please bring your MDC card and car registration. Get more information on parking decals.
Employees can pick up a parking decal in the Kendall Campus Public Safety Department office, located on campus in room 5118.
Please note: Parking decal is good for one academic year.
Visitors must obtain a parking pass from Public Safety, located on the south side of building 5. The visitor pass is to be displayed on the dash of the vehicle and visible for inspection.
Lot 14, located directly in front of the main entrance, is the visitor’s parking lot.
Hours  Monday-Friday: 6:00am-11:00pm  Saturday: 6:00am-7:00pm  Sunday: Closed
Students and part-time employees are allowed to park in the non-gated lots on the campus, including the open areas of the parking garage. The parking lots are numbered 1 – 16. The numbered signage is located on the light poles throughout the lots.
Lot 14, located directly in front of the main entrance, is reserved for campus visitors.
Hours  Monday-Friday: 6:00am-11:00pm  Saturday: 6:00am-7:00pm  Sunday: Closed
Faculty and full-time staff are allowed to park in gated areas on the campus, including the gated area of the parking garage.  
Hours  Monday-Friday: 6:00am-11:00pm  Saturday: 6:00am-7:00pm  Sunday: Closed
