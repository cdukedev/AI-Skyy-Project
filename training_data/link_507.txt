FAFSA Application Deadlines
2023- 2024 Academic Year

For federal aid, application must be submitted no later than June 30, 2024
Corrections or updates must be submitted by September 14, 2024.

2024- 2025 Academic Year

Available December 31, 2023
For state aid, application must be processed by May 15, 2024
For federal aid, application must be submitted no later than June 30, 2025
Corrections or updates must be submitted by September 14, 2025.
