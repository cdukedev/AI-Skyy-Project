Explore the Programs at Miami Dade College
Graduates of the BSN program will be eligible to apply for post-baccalaureate certificates and Master of Science in Nursing programs. They will also be able to provide nursing care in a variety of health care settings, develop innovative nursing practices, advance nursing as a profession and serve as exemplary role models for excellence in global nursing practice. 
A Bachelor of Science in Nursing degree (BSN) expands opportunities in various areas.
A Bachelor of Science in Nursing degree (BSN) prepares students for graduate education in various advanced practice areas.
