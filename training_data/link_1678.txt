Welcome to the Division of Human Resources.  We are committed to MDC’s mission and vision, continuously making MDC an employer of choice. We do this by focusing on:
Our Mission  To recruit, retain and develop a highly competent, diverse workforce that supports the mission, vision and values of MDC
Our Vision  To serve as the catalyst for MDC employees’ commitment and success through core values that reflect our everyday actions

For the tenth consecutive year, Miami Dade College has been named among the nation's Great Colleges to Work For by The Chronicle of Higher Education, a major higher education publication. The college is the only public higher education institution in South Florida named to the list.
