Connecting Credentials for Academic and Career Success
In the fields of public safety and criminal justice, there is one industry certificate in Basic Law Enforcement that stacks into a college credit certificate in Crime Scene Technician and one college credit certificate in Homeland Security. Both college credit certificates stack into the AS degree in Criminal Justice Technology and lead into a BAS degree in Public Safety Management.
