This annual scholarship is awarded to a K-12 Teacher Education program completer from Kendall Campus. The award is made during Honors Convocation annually in the spring.
This is a collaborative, performance-based scholarship program for African-American, Hispanic-American, Asian-American, and Native American baccalaureate students in teacher education programs. The minority teacher education scholars program shall provide an annual scholarship. Contact your professor. This grant requires a commitment of teaching equal to the number of years that the grant is received.
