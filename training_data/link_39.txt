
                    As part of our efforts to help you get certified at the minimum cost possible, we are reimbursing all students enrolled in a college-level or vocational credit course that paid and passed the certification exam.
                

                As an incentive, Miami Dade College may reimburse the cost of an approved industry examination to students who receive an industry certification found on the Industry Certification Funding List or approved initiative. 
To seek reimbursement through the School of Continuing Education &  Professional Education, please complete the Industry Certification CNA  Reimbursement Request Form.

              To seek reimbursement through the School of Education, please  complete the Florida Certificate  Application Reimbursement Form.
