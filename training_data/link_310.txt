Navigate is designed to help you manage your academic and non-academic lives.
Through Navigate, you may access a full range of activities and resources available at the College. You may access your to-do list and other time sensitive tasks while getting personalized guidance early and often. These “to-dos” will guide you through enrollment, connecting with your advisor, paying for classes, and more.
Download the Navigate mobile app on Google Play or the App Store.


Email us at navigate@mdc.edu or visit the New Student Center at your campus.
