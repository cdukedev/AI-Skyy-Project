Connecting Credentials for Academic and Career Success
In the field of industry manufacturing and construction, there are five industry certificates which stack into two college credit certificates in Computer Aided Design Assistant or Computer Aided Design Operator and an AS degree in Building Construction Technology.  In the area of transportation and logistics, three industry certificates will count toward two college credit certificates in Intermodal Freight Transportation or International Freight Transportation and an AS degree in Transportation & Logistics leading to a BAS degree in Supply Chain Management.
Please contact an academic advisor to plan and map stackable credentials for this program.
