Center for Institutional and Organizational Learning
To search and register for current workshops, log on to MDConnect and follow this path:
Employee Self Service>Learning>Find Learning
The best way to search for an existing workshop is to search by title. Any word in the title of the workshop will bring up the listing. Some common keywords include: Canvas, MDConnect, Advisement, Harassment, FERPA, etc.
 
To view your Learning transcript and print completion certificates:
View record of Learning completed before May of 2016.
Don't see what you are looking for? Fill out a Workshop Request Form and a representative from CIOL will get in touch with you.
