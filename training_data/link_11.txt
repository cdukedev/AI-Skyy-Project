Money Back Guarantee is a tuition refund program that helps students who complete any of the eligible workforce education programs and are not employed within six months after graduation. To be eligible for a tuition refund, students must meet all program requirements.
Submit Online Application
E: moneyback@mdc.eduP: 305-237-2120
Eligible students will be refunded the full cost of tuition for an eligible workforce education program.
The full tuition reimbursement of only one eligible workforce education program.
To be deemed eligible, the student must complete all the student eligibility criteria and apply for the Money-Back Guarantee Program no later than the first day of the month, 13 months after their official graduation.
Contact us at moneyback@mdc.edu or fill out the inquiry form on the website.
