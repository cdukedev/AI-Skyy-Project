
                        Employers across the country are seeing a skills gap when it comes to hiring new workers. Industry
                        certifications are addressing those needs and helping to build a skilled workforce.
                    

                          Get industry certification training through our credit and non-credit courses.
                        Get credits for certifications that you already obtained.
                          
                        Get prepared for EnTec related industry certification exams.
                      
Courses provide you with preparation materials, vouchers to take the certification exam for free, or reimbursement if you already paid and passed the certification while studying at MDC.
Students who are enrolled in a college-level or vocational credit course and pass the certification exam, will be reimbursed for the cost of the exam.
Get credits at MDC if you already have an active industry certification or licensure that aligns with a course from one of our A.S. programs.
