Connecting Credentials for Academic and Career Success
In the field of emergency medical services, an Emergency Medical Technician industry certificate will yield credit in two college credit certificates in Emergency Medical Technician or Paramedic and an AS degree in Emergency Medical Services. In health information technology, two industry certificates will count toward a college credit certificate in Health Informatics Specialist and an AS degree in Health Information Technology. A Florida Funeral Director college credit certificate stacks into the AS degree in Funeral Services.
