Miami Dade College has partnered with United Way Miami to provide scholarships for students and future professionals in the South Florida healthcare community. Through this partnership, eligible students may receive FREE tuition, books, fees, uniform and a stipend. Scholarships are funded through United Way Miami’s workforce fund, UpSkill Miami.
