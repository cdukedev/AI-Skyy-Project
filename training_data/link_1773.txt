
Dr. Sergio Gonzalez (Music) serves as  advisor to the Hialeah Campus Performing Arts Club. Dr. Gonzalez collaborates  with students in organizing performances where they have the opportunity to  develop and showcase to the community their unique artistic talents. Students  practice and perform for two annual on-campus concerts, where members of the  Miami-Dade College community enjoy performances including acting, singing, and  the playing of musical instruments. 
 Students also volunteer their musical  passion by participating in two annual performances at Epworth Villa, a local  retirement center. Additionally, singers of the club have also assisted with  the production of the multi-disciplinary event Hamilton in Hialeah. For information on how to join HPAC, please  contact Dr. Gonzalez at sgonzal5@mdc.edu.
