Explore the Programs at Miami Dade College
This RN to BSN program is offered 100% online and in a blended format upon demand. (No more than 30% live sessions, and up to 79% remote.)
The following tracks are options for beginning and progressing through the Bachelor of Science in Nursing. These options are designed to accommodate various student needs. They are not different degree programs.
The seamless transition track is designed for students who are graduating from the MDC Associate of Science in Nursing (ASN) degree program and have made application to the RN-BSN program and are scheduled to take their NCLEX RN examination within the semester of acceptance to the RN-BSN program.
 Once students have successfully completed  the NCLEX RN and present a copy of a Florida RN license to the New Student  Center or the RN-BSN Chairperson they will be recoded into the RN-BSN program.
 Students  who have not successfully completed the NCLEX RN by the end of the first  semester will not be permitted to progress in the BSN program.
This track is designed for students who desire to complete the program in three semesters. This track is recommended for those who are not working or are only working part-time.
