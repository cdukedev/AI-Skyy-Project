Students are enrolled into the Paramedic program each semester. You must successfully complete the required courses before progressing to discipline courses. 
The Emergency Medical Services  Department provides two offerings for paramedic enrollment.  These include  an offering according to the shift schedule used by various Miami Dade County  Fire/Rescue municipalities.  These classes are offered from 8:00am until  9:00pm each shift day exclude weekends.
            
This class is offered once a year in the Fall Semester only. Classes are held Monday through Thursday from 5:00pm until 9:00pm alternating clinical rotations and lecture classes during these days. Laboratory classes are scheduled every Saturday from 8:00am until 4:00pm.
