The Hialeah Campus Testing and Assessment Department offers a variety of assessments. Among these assessments are college placement tests, competency exams, entrance exams, professional certification exams, Pearson VUE assessments, Comira assessments, Distance Learning exams and student's feedback scanning. As well as surveys and services for non-MDC assessments.
The Hialeah Campus Testing Center is comprised of two testing labs with 59 stations, which can be used for computerized testing and/or paper-and-pencil testing. The Testing and Assessment Department Center also offers ACCESS accommodated testing for most examinations.
