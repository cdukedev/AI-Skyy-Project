Connecting Credentials for Academic and Career Success
In the field of computer information and database technology, there are eight industry certificates which yield credits toward seven college credit certificates and stack into four AS degrees in Database Technology-Microsoft Database Administrator, Database Technology-Oracle Database Administrator, Database Technology-Oracle Database Developer, or Computer Information Technology. Each AS degree leads into a BS in Information Systems Technology.
