The Kendall Campus Testing & Assessment Center offers a variety of assessments. Among these assessments are college placement tests, EAP and ESOL placement tests, prior learning assessments, high stakes exams, Pearson VUE assessments, certification testing, student feedback scanning, and proctoring services for non-MDC assessments. 
The Kendall Campus Testing & Assessment Center is comprised of four testing rooms with 188 testing stations, which can be used for computerized testing and/or paper-and-pencil testing. In addition, the Center also offers ACCESS accommodated testing for most examinations.
