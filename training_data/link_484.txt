Student Financial Services at Miami Dade College is committed to deliver exceptional customer service in providing accurate student financial information, individualized guidance, and support for students and families in an environment which embraces teamwork and collaborative partnership.
The education and financial records of Miami Dade College students and applicants are 
                    protected from disclosure under federal law [the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA)], 
                    and Florida law (Sections 1002.225 
                    and 1006.52 of the Florida Statutes). 
                    Pursuant to these laws, 
                    records maintained by the College that are directly related to a student or contain the student's 
                    personally identifiable information are confidential with a few exceptions.
