Miami Dade College promotes sustainability practices that contribute to a healthy planet, and embraces its responsibility to preserve the natural environment.
MDC educates students, staff, and community to be contributors to a healthy planet.
Through courses, workshops, fairs and more, the College fosters a deeper respect for Earth and all its inhabitants.
MDC addresses environmental sustainability in all operations of the college to promote a positive impact on the environment.
From the ground-up construction to the way we move and meet, sustainable practices are a part of everything we do at the College.
