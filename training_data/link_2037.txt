Professional cybersecurity training for organizations and individuals



Maximize your team’s training experience by having them face real-time cyberattacks inside our state-of-the-art S.O.C. facility.
Our training center simulates the look and feel of a real Security Operations Center with top-of-the-line technology throughout. From video walls to laser projectors to smart whiteboards, the team will feel as if they are in a true command center when dealing with advanced cyberattacks.
Each individual will have their own professional workspace with access to industry-leading security tools such as firewalls, SIEMs, IDS/IPS systems, packet analyzers, network monitoring systems, etc.
Whether an individual is a Tier 1 S.O.C. Analyst or an Advanced Incident Responder, training within a realistic S.O.C. will provide an extra level of realism that will truly enhance the training experience.
