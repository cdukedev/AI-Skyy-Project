The Padrón Campus Testing Center provides placement and assessment services for Miami Dade College students, administrators, faculty, and the community at large to make informed educational decisions. Our services include, but are not limited to, placement tests, professional certification tests, Pearson VUE assessments, prior learning assessments, virtual college proctored tests, high stakes exams such as SAT and ACT, and student feedback.
The Padrón Campus Testing Center is comprised of two testing rooms with 38 testing stations which can be used for computerized or paper and pencil testing. The center also has ACCESS testing accommodation spaces.
