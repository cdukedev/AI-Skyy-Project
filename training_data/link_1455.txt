The Facilities Planner is responsible for all facilities planning activities. Responsibilities of this office include developing educational specifications in conjunction with the campus special projects. Office responsibilities include maintaining the Campus priority lists for new construction, remodeling and renovation projects; serving as the Campus liaison with State facilities survey teams and meeting with Campus President and Administrators to aid them in planning Campus facilities priority list.
When restoring or upgrading an area, i.e. renovating or remodeling, the Facilities Planner coordinates personnel moves, also plans and coordinates with the Campus scheduling office or classroom availability during the project period. All facilities problems and items are channeled through the facilities planner office for action, discussion and implementation.
