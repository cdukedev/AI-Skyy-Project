Students and part-time employees and can pick up a parking decal from Student Life Office located on campus in Building A and full-time employees can pick up a parking decal at the Homestead Campus Public Safety Department office located on campus in Building D.
Students and part-time employees can park in both non-gated parking lots, located on the East and West of the college. There are 569 parking spaces for students and part-time employees.
Hours:  Monday - Friday 7:00am to 10:00pm  Saturday 7:00am to 5:00pm  Sunday Closed
Full-time employees can park in the gated East parking lot. There are 569 parking spaces for full-time employees.
Hours:  Monday - Friday 7:00am to 10:00pm  Saturday 7:00am to 5:00pm  Sunday Closed
