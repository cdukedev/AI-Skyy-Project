Connecting Credentials for Academic and Career Success
In the field of advanced manufacturing, an MSSC industry certificate will stack into two college credit certificates in Automation and Lean Manufacturing which lead to an AS degree in Industrial Engineering Technology. In the area of horticulture, the Horticulture Professional industry certificate stacks into two college credit certificates and an AS degree in Landscape and Horticulture Technology. In biotechnology, there is one college credit certificate in Biotechnology leading to an AS degree in Biotechnology and stacks into three advanced technical certificates in Bioinformatics, Biotechnology, and Chemical Technology leading into a BS degree in Biological Sciences-Biotechnology.
