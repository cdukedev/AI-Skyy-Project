The Meek Center is a major outreach center of the North Campus that has a particular focus on educational opportunities in entrepreneurship, business-related programs, technology, community development, and vocational programs. It's a small and friendly learning environment where students can obtain essential skills that will position them as in-demand candidates in today's job market.
Additionally, the Center has a full range of student and academic support services. for students who want to avoid long lines at other locations. Students can use the Center to complete academic programs, get their general education requirements for associate degrees, or as a convenient additional location for classes.The Meek Center also offers opportunities to engage in a variety of seminars, conferences, and workshops.
