Explore the Programs at Miami Dade College
The RN-BSN program is offered fully online and has a part time option that may appeal to the working nurse. In addition, the program may be offered via the blended format approximately 30% synchronous and 70 % asynchronous, based on demand. For the last three years, 73% of students have graduated within the expected graduation time which is above 70%. The administration, faculty and staff work together to encourage students to complete the program by offering a supportive learning environment with testing preparation for NCLEX success.
Satisfaction surveys completed by the RN-BSN program graduates reveal a high satisfaction with the program. Student surveys from 2021-2022 indicated that the students were both satisfied and very satisfied with the RN-BSN Program. Most of the program graduates rate the program most favorable and would recommend the program to other students.
