Connecting Credentials for Academic and Career Success
In the field of entrepreneurship, there are four college credit certificates that stack into three AS degrees in Business Entrepreneurship or Marketing Management and lead into a BAS in Supervision and Management. A college credit certificate in Accounting Technology Management stacks into an AS degree in Accounting Technology and also leads into a BAS in Supervision and Management.
