Enrollment verification provides proof of your academic status.
Why do I need to provide enrollment verification?

        This formal verification may be of use to you in matters of housing, income tax, financial aid, employment, loans and so on.
How do I get the verification?

        Verifications are available after the 100% refund period of the term and classes you are taking. Once it’s available, you may print your individualized enrollment verification, including the good student discount option 24 hours a day/7 days a week.
How much does it cost?
        There is no charge for this service.
