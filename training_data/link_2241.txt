Your opinion matters
Within Watermark's Course Evaluations & Surveys (CES, formerly EvaluationKIT), Miami Dade College's online student feedback platform, 
  faculty and authorized administrators have direct access to real-time response rates, results and more. 
   Course Evaluations & Surveys (CES, formerly EvaluationKIT) includes other features, such as a report builder to look at results over 
  time and across courses. 
Please use the options below to access  Student Feedback via  Course Evaluations & Surveys (CES, formerly EvaluationKIT).

  Emails sent from ‘MDC Student Feedback’ contains a Login  Link. This link is unique to each recipient and does not require a  password. Do not forward this email. The Login Link will send you  directly to your  Course Evaluations & Surveys (CES, formerly EvaluationKIT) dashboard. These emails are easily recognizable  since they are sent with the following descriptors below. 
  From: AET@mdc.edu
  Name:  MDC Student Feedback
If you  have any questions, please contact District Assessment, Evaluation and Testing  via AET@mdc.edu or the Campus Assistance.
