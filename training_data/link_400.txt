The Wolfson Campus Testing Center offers a variety of assessments. Among these assessments are college placement tests, EAP and ESOL placement tests, prior learning assessments, high stakes exams, Pearson VUE assessments, certification testing, student feedback scanning, and proctoring services for non-MDC assessments.
The Wolfson Campus Testing Center is comprised of four testing rooms with 172 testing stations, which can be used for computerized testing (110 stations) and/or paper-and-pencil testing (172 stations). The Center also offers ACCESS accommodated testing for most examinations.
