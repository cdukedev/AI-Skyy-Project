Explore the Programs at Miami Dade College
The 9-credit-hour certificate program in Business Industry Operations, includes a series of courses that offer students the technical knowledge and skills needed to prepare for further education and careers in a variety of industries aligned to a student’s pathway.
Courses can be completed in one or two semesters.
The majority of these courses can be taken evenings and weekends.
Students will obtain a college credit certificate.
Preparation for further education and careers in a variety of industries aligned to a student’s pathway.
This program takes one year or less.
This certificate is designed to serve the needs of students who are presently employed in business operations, as well as those students who are interested in entering this industry. College credits granted with this certificate apply toward a related A.S. degree. Students may stack credentials to advance toward a higher degree level.
