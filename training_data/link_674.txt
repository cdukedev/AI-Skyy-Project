In compliance with Violence Against Women Act Re-authorization Act of 2013 (VaWa) and the Jeanne Clery Disclosure Act (Clery Act), Miami Dade College proactively educates its student body regarding issues of sexual assault and methods of prevention. Students can find useful information, community resources and make confidential reports at the Sexual Violence Prevention site inside the student portal.
For more information about sexual assault prevention at MDC, contact the Office of Sexual Violence Prevention at prevention@mdc.edu or 305-237-2663.
