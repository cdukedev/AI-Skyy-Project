Connecting Credentials for Academic and Career Success
In the field of hospitality, there are six college credit certificates that stack into the AS degree in Hospitality & Tourism Management and leads to a BAS in Supervision and Management. In the area of culinary arts, there are three industry certifications which yield credits toward two college credit certificates and an AS in Culinary Arts Management.
