Student Government Association (SGA) is the voice of the student body. 
                        We serve as the student governing body responsible for advocating for 
                        the students and representing students' interests and addressing students'
                        needs with the campus leadership. As SGA members, we promote student 
                        involvement and offer leadership, organizational, and communication 
                        skills training, while providing the opportunities to attend conferences, 
                        retreats and special meetings to exchange ideas with other campuses and 
                        colleges across the State of Florida.
