The office of Private Sector Security provides State of Florida certified training to individuals interested in obtaining the “D” Security Officer license, the “CC” Private Investigator Intern license, and the  “G” Statewide Firearm License. Only Class “C,” Class “CC,” Class "D," Class “M,” Class “MA” and Class "MB" licensees are permitted to bear a firearm, and any such licensee who bears a firearm must also have a Class “G” license. We also offer the Mandatory “G” requalification course.
Our instructors are state certified and have many years of experience in law enforcement, investigations and emergency services.
