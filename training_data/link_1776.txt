
Professor Rodolfo Cruz serves as the advisor for the Society of  Hispanic Professional Engineers at the Hialeah Campus. He has been recognized  as the Advisor Super Star award for Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers  Region 7 (Region 7 includes Florida, Georgia, and the Carolinas).  
					        The club participates in competitions where students put their  knowledge, talent and perseverance to the test while competing  shoulder-to-shoulder with other students from all over the country.
