With a goal of educating staff, students, faculty and the community to be contributors to a healthy planet, the College now offers more than 30 sustainability-focused courses through the School of Continuing Education and Professional development. From energy conservation to healthier eating, these courses foster a deeper respect for Earth and life in all its diversity.
