Admissions and support information for international students
The State of Florida Legislature established 11 International  Linkage Institutes to develop stronger economic, cultural, educational and  social ties between Florida and countries considered strategically important to  the state (Florida state statute 288.8175). The Linkage Institutes'  Out-of-State Tuition Exemption program provides international students from Brazil,  Canada, Caribbean, China, Costa Rica, Eastern Europe, France, Israel, Japan,  Mexico and West Africa with the opportunity to apply for a waiver of the  out-of-state portion of tuition.
