Explore the Programs at Miami Dade College
Miami Dade College School  of Justice, Public Safety and Law Studies has been the premiere provider of  security training for 29 years. Our instructors are state certified and have  many years of work experience in law enforcement, investigations, private  security and emergency services. 
The Private Security  Officer program consists of two courses; a 42 hour Basic Security Guard  Training and 28 hour Private Security Guard Training 2. The Basic Phase A  course allows the officer to apply for a Florida Class D Security License.  Students must complete the Basic Phase B course in order to apply for a G  statewide firearms license.

The office of Private  Sector Security provides State of Florida certified training to individuals  interested in obtaining the “D” Security Officer license, the “CC” Private  Investigator Intern license, the “G” license for “D”, “CC” and “C” license  holders desiring to work armed and mandatory requalification courses.
