Thanks to dedicated leadership at many levels, Miami Dade College has evolved over the years from its humble beginnings as a social experiment into the globally recognized educational institution it is today. MDC welcomes all students and invites them to share their very best so that along with staff and faculty, we can all contribute to the ultimate success of this institution and our community.
Madeline Pumariega is the first female president to lead Miami Dade College, the institution she attended decades earlier as a student-athlete.
