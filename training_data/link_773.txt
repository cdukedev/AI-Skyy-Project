Welcome to the Miami Dade College Hialeah  Campus!  Thank you for your interest in  our dynamic campus.  The Hialeah Campus  is a modern state of the art campus that prides itself on valuing the  individual and having a welcoming family environment. The faculty and staff  members of the Hialeah Campus are committed to delivering a premier college  experience by using a students first approach. We are eager to serve learners  of all ages and assist them with their educational journey. In addition, our  campus strives to engage community and industry partners to provide the most  relevant academic programs. We are very grateful for the opportunity to serve  the greater Hialeah area.
I invite you to join our Hialeah Campus  Family/Familia today!
