Admissions and support information for international students
Thank you for your interest in attending Miami Dade College (MDC). The admission requirements section contains very important information regarding your admission to the College. Please read all of the information carefully. Please review all of the necessary forms and paperwork that must be submitted prior to the issuance of the I-20. Once all required documents have been received, the I-20 will be completed within 4 weeks. Please adhere to term deadline.
Ready to take the first step? Applying to the College is quick and easy. Get started today.
If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact the International Students Services Office on the campus to which you are applying, and they will be happy to assist you.
