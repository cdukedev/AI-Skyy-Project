The Office of Campus Administration oversees Facilities Rental & Room Reservations which coordinates the reservation of several rooms at the Wolfson Campus. Our venues are used for meetings, conferences and other educationally related events for different departments throughout the College, as well as for the community we serve. Regardless of your event needs, the Wolfson Campus’ vast array of facilities and on-site personnel can ensure your events success. We are confident that our diverse facilities can accommodate your event needs.
