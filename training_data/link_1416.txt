In accordance with state mandated annual training and to enhance the skills of law enforcement and correctional practitioners, the School of Justice, Public Safety and Law studies offers specialized training programs based on assessed needs determined collectively by their staff, the Miami Dade Chiefs of Police Association and industry trends.
On average the Career Development Section each month presents 5 career development programs. On the average 1500 officers attend classes such as computer facilitated crimes, terrorist tactics, active shooter response, interview techniques and developing strategies to resolve community issues. 
For information about our career and professional development courses, please fill out our Region XIV form, and also visit our scheduling page for a listing of other courses offered by the Florida Department of Law Enforcement.
