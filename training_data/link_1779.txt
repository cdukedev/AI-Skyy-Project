The mission of the Hialeah Campus Art Gallery is to encourage appreciation and understanding of art and its role in society through direct engagement with original works of art. The Gallery exhibits make accessible works of art for the benefit of a variety of audiences, in support of the interdisciplinary educational goals of the college and for the benefit of the community at large.
