The mission of the Wolfson Campus Media Services Department is to serve the needs of faculty, administrators, staff and students with an array of multimedia resources that enhance teaching and learning experiences, campus marketing, and campus life. Our production and special event team is ready to support all campus events, video production, and classroom audio-visual needs as well as provide assistance with lecture capture, photography, audio, and videoconferencing.
Please feel free to contact the Media Services Department for all the services we can provide.
