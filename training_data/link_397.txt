Miami Dade College (MDC) North Campus Testing Center is committed to enhancing student success by providing a wide range of comprehensive and accessible testing services. Our services include, but are not limited to college placement testing, competency testing, professional certifications, and student feedback. The Testing Center also provides testing services for students enrolled in our Virtual College program and distance learning courses at other institutions.
Our priority is providing students with the proper guidance and tools they need to prepare for their exams and ultimately reach their academic goals.
The North Campus Testing Center is comprised of four testing rooms with 109 testing stations, which can be used for computerized and/or paper-and-pencil testing. The Center also offers ACCESS accommodated testing for most of the examinations.
