Connecting Credentials for Academic and Career Success
In the field of education, the National Child Development Association industry certificate will yield credits in three college credit certificates in Early Childhood Education - Administrator, Infant-Toddler, and Pre-school stacking into four AS degrees in Early Childhood Education-Administrator, Early Childhood Education-Infant-Toddler, Early Childhood Education-Preschool, or Early Childhood Education-General which leads to a BS degree in Early Childhood Education.
