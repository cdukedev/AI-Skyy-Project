Vehicle usage is governed by college procedure 1470. The procedure provides a streamlined process for authorized employees to use College vehicles.
In order to use a College vehicle, employees must complete the forms below and confirm driver authorization by Risk Management and/or attend a required driver training session.
To reserve an MDC vehicle for official business only, please use the electronic form below to submit your request to the Public Safety administration office for availability 2 weeks in advance.
Reservations must be made a week in advance by e-mailing the Vehicle Reservations form to northvehicleuse@mdc.edu.
For more information, email westvehicleuse@mdc.edu to obtain further details regarding your reservation or request.
For more information, email kendallvehicleuse@mdc.edu to obtain further details regarding your reservation or request.
