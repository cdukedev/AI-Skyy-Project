The Digital Marketing Specialist Certificate is designed to prepare students to build and execute a marketing plan using digital tools to design, distribute, promote, and price a product or service. Graduates will understand how to connect with consumers using multiple digital platforms to create effective targeted promotional campaigns. 
This certificate is designed to serve the needs of  students who are presently employed in the field of marketing, as well as those  students who are interested in this field.
