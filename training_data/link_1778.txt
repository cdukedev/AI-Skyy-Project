The mission of the Campus Support  Services Department at the Hialeah Campus is to serve the needs of the campus, in the areas of Custodial, Duplicating, Mail, Reservations and Event Planning  services, Facilities and the Information Desk. We strive to provide quality  services that enhance the learning environments for students, faculty, staff  and community members.
  
Campus Services Department is responsible for the following areas: Custodial Services, Duplicating (Quick Copy Center), Mailroom, Maintenance, Reservations & Event Planning Services, and the Wellness Lounge.
