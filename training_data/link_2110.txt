Connecting Credentials for Academic and Career Success
In the field of computer programming and network technology, there are several industry certifications leading to seven college credit certificates and stack into four AS degrees in Network Services Technology, Computer Programming & Analysis-Business Applications Programming, Computer Programming & Analysis-Mobile Applications Development or Computer Engineering Technology.  The AS degree in Network Services Technology leads into the BS in Information Systems Technology.
