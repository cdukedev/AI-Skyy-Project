Welcome to the Custodial Services web page. As part of the Division of Administrative Services, we are committed to providing an atmosphere allowing students, faculty and staff at Miami Dade College, Wolfson Campus to meet their goals. We are committed to make a positive contribution to the total academic experience and to the growth and development of the College and its programs. 
Custodial Services provides a high level of quality cleaning throughout the campus, meeting the needs of students, faculty, and staff, maintaining a clean, healthy, and safe learning environment.
If any issues need to be reported, please contact Custodial Service MAIN HOTLINE at 305-237-3411.
If your request is related to an event, please submit an Event Setup Work order.
