Maria Bosque-Blanco is a guidance counselor at Our Lady of Lourdes Academy in Miami. She is nationally certified in school psychology and was previously a school psychologist in Broward County Public Schools. Trustee Bosque-Blanco is a member of the National Association for College Admission Counseling. She attended Miami Dade College and earned her bachelor’s and master’s degrees in school psychology from Barry University.
