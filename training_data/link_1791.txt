Miami Dade College's Medical Campus offers quality medical care  through two clinics: Vision Care Clinic, and the Dental Hygiene Clinic.  Services are provided at minimal costs.
				      In addition to this critical health-care role, the clinics also  serve another purpose: They provide the students at Medical Campus with  indispensable real-world clinical experience under the guidance of our  distinguished faculty and partners.
